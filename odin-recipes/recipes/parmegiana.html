Parmegiana

Description

Parmigiana is a classic Italian dish made with thinly sliced, floured, and fried chicken layered with tomato sauce and cheese, and baked in the oven.

Ingredients

  • chicken breast
  • salt and pepper
  • eggs
  • panko bread crumbs
  • parmesan cheese and mozarella cheese
  • flour
  • olive oil and frying oil
  • tomato sauce

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 450 degrees
  2. Season chicken and evenly coat both sides with flour
  3. Dip chicken into beated eggs and then dip into bread crumbs
  4. Fry chicken in pan for 2 minutes each side
  5. Cover chicken with tomato sauce and cheese
  6. Place chicken in oven for 15-20 minutes
