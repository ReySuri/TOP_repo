Steak

Description

A steak is a thick cut of meat generally sliced across the muscle fibers, sometimes including a bone. It is normally grilled or fried.

Ingredients

  • steak
  • salt and pepper
  • olive oil
  • butter

Steps

  1. Pour olive oil and butter into pan
  2. Season meat and when the pan is hot enough throw meat into pan
  3. Cook meat on both sides for 2 minutes, then lower temperature to medium and cook on each side for 1 minute until doneness is as desired
